About Us

Rajokari Institute of Technology (RIT) is Established by Department of Training & Technical Education under the MHRD scheme in August 2016 with two courses:

• Computer Engineering
• Information Technology Enabled Services and Management (ITESM)

RIT has set its goal on churning out technocrats of excellence.
